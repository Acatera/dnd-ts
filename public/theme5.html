Theme 5: Neon Terminal

This theme is inspired by vibrant neon colors on a dark terminal-like background. It's bold and flashy, perfect for a futuristic setting.

Why this theme?

Perfect for immersive text-based games, where the player feels like they are interacting with a high-tech interface from a cyberpunk world.

Links like this one stand out brightly, and buttons offer a neon-lit experience.
